Purrfectly Yours - Client Information

Purrfectly Yours is a startup specialising in personalised D2C pet accessories, gifts, and toys.

In today's pet product market, there's a noticeable gap when it comes to truly unique and high-quality customized pet items. Generic, mass-produced pet products just don't capture the individuality of our beloved pets.

The UK pet market is thriving, with accessories estimated at a value between £0.9-1.5 billion. In a nation where 12 million households are proud pet owners, the total pet care market is valued at a staggering £6.5 billion, and it's still growing at 4% annually.

However, the pet accessories market is largely dominated by industry giants like Pets at Home and others who flood the market with mass-produced, lower-quality goods. Our smaller competition make high-quality for offline distributors, who take significant cuts of the profits.

This leaves a big gap for personalized and high-quality pet accessories. That's where Purrfectly Yours positions itself.

Their growth estimates are ambitious. By the end of the year, they aim to achieve £1 million in sales, and we're off to a promising start. We want you to help us review our business sales data, and find opportunities for us to grow as we come into Q4.
